About Me

I graduated from Emory University in 2014 with a Finance & Accounting degree and spent 5 years working on Wall Street. Although I was having a “successful” career by most worldly standards, deep down, it did not bring me the joy and fulfillment I wanted out of my profession. That realization set me down a different path.

I left Wall Street altogether, becoming a Sales Engineer at a data analytics software startup called Looker (a subsidiary of Google Cloud). I spent two years growing my technical skills (SQL, Python, and Javascript), but wanted more. That led to where I am today - pursuing a formal computer science degree and aspiring to become a software engineer.
